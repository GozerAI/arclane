## Section 1: Hacker News "Show HN" Post

**Title:** Show HN: Arclane -- AI incubator that runs your startup's first 90 days

**Body:**

I built Arclane because I kept watching solo founders spend weeks on strategy docs, market research, and landing pages before writing a single line of product code. Arclane compresses that into automated daily execution cycles.

Here is what actually happens: you describe your business idea, and within the first cycle you get four concrete deliverables -- a strategy brief, a market research report, a landing page draft, and a launch tweet. Not templates. Each is generated from your specific idea, market, and competitive landscape.

From there, Arclane runs one execution cycle per day for 90 days across four phases: Foundation (Days 1-21, positioning and initial content), Validation (Days 22-45, market testing and iteration), Growth (Days 46-70, distribution and revenue tracking), and Scale-Ready (Days 71-90, operations and systems). Each cycle produces a real deliverable -- competitive intel reports, content pieces, distribution channel setup, revenue tracking.

The stack is Python/FastAPI with async SQLAlchemy. Each business gets its own subdomain on arclane.cloud and runs in an isolated container. Nightly cycles pull from Trendscope (trend intelligence) and a knowledge harvester for market data. Health scoring tracks business progress across dimensions and adapts task selection based on gaps.

After Day 90, an adaptive optimizer continues running cycles weighted by what your business actually needs.

This is a solo project by me at 1450 Enterprises. I am genuinely looking for feedback on the execution model -- whether daily automated deliverables are useful or just noise.

Day 1 is free — no card required. Choose a plan to keep building, with a 48-hour trial to cancel.

https://arclane.cloud
