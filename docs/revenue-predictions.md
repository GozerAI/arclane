## 7. Break-Even Analysis

### Operating Costs (Monthly, Bootstrap Mode)

| Cost | Monthly | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Infrastructure (hosting, DB, Redis) | $125 | Scales with users — estimate for 500 users |
| AI API costs (all users) | $460 | ~500 users × $0.92 COGS |
| Marketing budget | $1,200 | Month 1–3 conservative; scales with revenue |
| Founder salary (partial) | $4,000 | Below-market; deferred comp model |
| Tools (analytics, email, monitoring) | $180 | Postmark, Sentry, Plausible, etc. |
| **Total Monthly Burn** | **$5,965** | Conservative bootstrap estimate |

### Break-Even Point

At $78 ARPU, break-even requires: $5,965 ÷ $78 = **77 paying users**

Under the base model, Arclane reaches 77 paid users during **Month 3 (June 2026)** — approximately 60 days after launch.

If founder salary is removed (deferred), break-even requires 26 paying users — achievable **Week 2 post-launch** if Product Hunt performs.

**Cash-flow positive (including founder salary): Month 3**
**Cash-flow positive (excluding deferred comp): Month 1**

## 8. Investment Case

### $10,000 Marketing Spend (12-Month Return)

Allocation: $2,500 Google Ads, $3,000 newsletter sponsorships (IH + Starter Story), $2,000 Twitter/LinkedIn paid, $1,500 cold email tooling (Apollo/Clay), $1,000 affiliate payouts.

Expected additional paid users attributable to spend (above organic baseline): ~95 users
At $78 ARPU × 12-month average lifespan of 14.8 months: LTV per acquired user = $1,154
Total LTV from spend: 95 × $1,154 = **$109,630**
**ROI on $10K: 10.96x — 12-month return of $109,630**

### $25,000 Marketing Spend (12-Month Return)

Allocation: $8,000 Google Ads (scale proven campaigns), $5,000 newsletter sponsorships (IH + SS + The Hustle), $4,000 LinkedIn Sponsored, $4,000 YouTube promotions, $3,000 affiliate/referral payouts, $1,000 cold email.

Expected additional paid users: ~290
Total LTV: 290 × $1,154 = **$334,660**
**ROI on $25K: 13.4x — 12-month return of $334,660**

### $50,000 Marketing Spend (12-Month Return)

Allocation: $18,000 Google Ads (broadening keyword set), $10,000 newsletters + podcast sponsorships, $8,000 LinkedIn + Twitter paid, $6,000 retargeting, $5,000 influencer/affiliate, $3,000 PR outreach.

Expected additional paid users: ~680 (diminishing returns on paid; organic compounding carries more weight)
Total LTV: 680 × $1,154 = **$784,720**
**ROI on $50K: 15.7x — 12-month return of $784,720**

**Key insight:** Because Arclane's gross margin is 98.8%, almost every dollar of revenue flows to profit after CAC recovery. The investment case improves the more budget is allocated to owned/earned channels (content, community) vs. paid-only, since LTV far exceeds CAC in all scenarios and the product has genuine word-of-mouth mechanics through the "incubator graduation" story arc.
