About

Built for founders who'd rather ship than shuffle

Arclane exists because running a business shouldn't mean drowning in operational busywork.

When you're building something new, every hour matters. But too much of that time gets eaten by things that aren't your product — writing blog posts, researching competitors, planning strategy, managing content, keeping the wheels turning.

Arclane automates the operational side of running a business. You describe what your business does and who it serves. Arclane takes it from there — creating content, researching your market, planning strategy, and delivering real, usable work to your dashboard every day.

What we believe

Small teams shouldn't need to hire a marketing department, a research analyst, and a content writer before they can compete. The right automation should make a team of one feel like a team of ten.

We also believe that AI tools should show their work, not hide behind a black box. That's why everything Arclane does is visible in your activity feed — you can see what's running, what's complete, and what it produced. No mystery, no hand-waving.

How we're different

Most business tools give you dashboards to stare at. Arclane gives you finished work to review. We're not another analytics platform or project management tool. We're the team that does the work while you focus on your customers.

Transparent

Every action is logged. Every result is visible. You always know what Arclane is doing and why.

Autonomous

Set it up once and it keeps running. No babysitting, no manual triggers, no constant check-ins.

Practical

We focus on output that matters — content you can publish, research you can act on, strategy you can follow.
