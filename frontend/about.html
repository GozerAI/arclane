About

An incubator that builds alongside you

Arclane is a structured AI incubator program — 90 days of daily execution that turns an idea into a real, measurable business.

Most AI tools give you a chatbot and call it a day. Arclane gives you a program — a structured 90-day path through Foundation, Validation, Growth, and Scale-Ready phases, with daily milestones and graduation criteria that tell you exactly where you stand.

You describe your business once. Arclane runs daily execution cycles that produce content, research competitors, track revenue, score your business health across five dimensions, and deliver advisory alerts when something needs attention. Every piece of work is tied to a phase and milestone in your roadmap.

What we believe

Starting a business shouldn't require hiring a marketing department, a research analyst, and a content writer before you can compete. A structured program with the right AI execution should make a solo founder as effective as a team of ten.

We also believe that AI tools should show their work. Everything Arclane does is visible in your activity feed and tied to your roadmap — you can see what's running, what phase it belongs to, and what it produced. No black box.

How we're different

Most AI business tools are open-ended — you prompt, they respond, nothing compounds. Arclane is a program with structure. Four phases. Daily milestones. Health scoring. Competitive intelligence. Revenue tracking. Phase graduation. And after Day 90, an adaptive optimizer that keeps building based on what your business actually needs.
