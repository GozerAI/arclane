Features

A structured incubator, not another AI tool

Arclane runs a 90-day program that moves your business through four phases — Foundation, Validation, Growth, and Scale-Ready — with daily milestones and real deliverables.

The 90-day program

1

Foundation (Days 1-20)

Strategy brief, market research, competitive landscape, landing page, first content, and business provisioning — all delivered through daily cycles.

  • Day 1 instant-value pack: strategy, research, landing page, launch tweet
  • Subdomain, email, and web presence provisioned automatically
  • Go-to-market positioning and offer framing
2

Validation (Days 21-45)

Content production ramps up, competitive monitoring kicks in, and early revenue signals get tracked.

  • Content repurposed into 8 formats automatically
  • Revenue tracking with source attribution
  • Health scoring across five business dimensions
3

Growth (Days 46-75)

Advisory system identifies gaps, competitive intelligence deepens, and content performance drives optimization.

  • Competitive monitoring with market signal analysis
  • Content analytics: views, clicks, and conversion tracking
  • Benchmarking against businesses at the same stage
4

Scale-Ready (Days 76-90)

Final push toward graduation. Revenue forecasting, growth experiments, and readiness assessment.

  • Graduation criteria checked automatically each cycle
  • Velocity tracking, ETA estimation, bottleneck detection
  • Phase advancement based on measurable progress
After Day 90: Graduated businesses enter the adaptive optimizer — ongoing cycles driven by health score gaps, market signals, and growth opportunities. No fixed curriculum, just intelligent maintenance.
