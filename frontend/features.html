Features

Built for the business ramp, not permanent backoffice sprawl

Arclane helps founders launch something new or stimulate an existing business through structured research, messaging, provisioning, and daily execution cycles.

What Arclane delivers

✎

Launch assets

Arclane turns raw business context into founder-ready materials you can actually use.

  • Business naming and offer framing
  • Mission, positioning, and homepage copy
  • Landing-page and messaging drafts
  • First social and content assets
🔎

Market research

Research is part of the core system so the business package reflects the market it has to enter.

  • Competitor landscape analysis
  • Customer and market signal review
  • Opportunity and wedge identification
  • Deeper research paths when needed
🎯

Strategy outputs

Arclane translates intake and research into a queue of business-building work instead of stopping at insight.

  • Go-to-market recommendations
  • Prioritized build queue
  • Offer and positioning refinements
  • Execution recommendations tied to the next step
🌐

Provisioned business surface

The product can turn outputs into visible business infrastructure instead of leaving them as disconnected drafts.

  • Custom subdomain on arclane.cloud
  • Starter web presence from templates
  • Automatic SSL and hosting
  • Business address configuration
⚙

Daily execution cycles

Work is spread across a queue so progress is visible and the ramp can continue over time.

  • Nightly execution cadence
  • Multi-day outputs for larger work
  • Visible queue progression
  • Add-ons that cut into line when purchased
💬

Plain-language control

Founders can still steer the system without having to learn internal agent structure.

  • Natural language task requests
  • Recommendations surfaced in context
  • Queue visibility without noise
  • Full history of completed outputs
