Frequently asked questions

What is a credit?

A credit is one execution cycle. Each cycle performs a complete round of work for your business — content creation, market research, strategy analysis, and more. One credit = one full cycle of autonomous work.

How does the free preview work?

Sign up with just your email and get 3 credits immediately. No credit card required. Use them to see real results from Arclane before committing to a paid plan.

Can I switch plans?

Yes. You can upgrade or downgrade at any time from your dashboard. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

What happens when I run out of credits?

Your business data and content are always available. You just won't be able to run new execution cycles until your credits renew or you purchase a credit pack.

How does the Starter trial work?

The Starter plan includes a 3-day trial period. We collect your card details upfront, but you won't be charged until the trial ends. Cancel anytime during the trial at no cost.

Can I manage multiple businesses?

Yes. The Growth plan supports up to 3 businesses and Scale supports up to 5. Each business gets its own subdomain, content library, and execution cycles.
